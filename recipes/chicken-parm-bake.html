Chicken Parmesan Bake

Description

A family pleasing dish that everyone will enjoy. This flavor packed dish will get everyone going for seconds.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Breast 3-4lbs
  • Pasta Sauce of choice 24oz
  • Rigatoni Pasta 12oz
  • Eggs 2
  • Flour 1/2cup
  • Mozzarella Cheese 8oz
  • Parmesan Cheese 4oz
  • Seasoned Salt for seasoning
  • Italian Breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
  • Vegatable Oil 2tbl
  • Butter 2oz
  • Fresh Parsley for ganish

Steps

  1. Set oven to 375
  2. Boil the Rigatoni, al dente is fine and drain
  3. While the pasta is boiling
  4. Slice the check breast into tiny strips and season it
  5. Combine the eggs and flour in bowl
  6. Pour the breadcumbs in another bowl
  7. Heat up pan with oil
  8. Coat the chicken pieces in the egg bowl first than the breadcumbs
  9. When the pan is hot enough place the chicken until golden brown about 5 minutes each side
  10. Combine your sauce and pasta
  11. Pour half the pasta in a baking dish
  12. Place half the chicken atop the pasta
  13. Add half of both cheeses
  14. Add the rest of the pasta with chicken and pasta
  15. Add parsley
  16. Bake uncovered for 25 minutes
