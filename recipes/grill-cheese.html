Grill Cheese

The following recipe is my take on the original grill cheese. This is a sample snack that you and your family will love. Best of all it specifically done within your air-fryer to limit the pots that need to be washed.

So no need to start your stove, just plug in your high tech air-fryer and follow along.

Ingredients

  • Pepperidge farm italian white bread
  • Butter, slightly melted for easy spreading
  • Yellow cheese, I get Old World Red Hoop Cheddar cheese(from Costco)

Steps

  1. Take 1/4 butter and place it on a microwavable bowl. You may need more depending on how many sandwiches your making
  2. Warm it up for about 7 seconds. You don't want it be water liquified just melted enough where you can easily spread it
  3. Spread butter on both sides of each bread slice
  4. Chop your cheese into slices, don't make them to thin because you want to taste the cheese of course
  5. Make sure the cheese covers the whole slice
  6. Bring together the other bread slice and place on the air-fryer

    7. Make sure you use a liner for your air-fryer so that it can be an easier clean up later

  7. Set air-fryer for 390
  8. Cook for 3 minutes on one side and 2 minutes when you flip
  9. Remove from air-fryer and cut diagonally only

You'll see that they are done once the bread has that nice brown color and the cheese is oozing a bit.

Side note

You can add a slice or two of your favorite ham in between the cheese to change it up a bit. This adds a bit of protein to the meal. If you get the Pepperidge farm bread it will also increase the amount of protein.
