Pancakes

This is my recipe for Kodiak pancakes. Its a simple one which I frequent weekend mornings. The Kodiak mix is my favorite due to the added protein. If you follow my recipe you'll have tasty pancakes which aren't too dry and will leave you feeling full.

My recipe makes about 6 decently sized pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Kodiak pancake mix
  • butter(for the pan)
  • 1.5 cups of 2%milk
  • 2 tablespoons of cinnamon powder or chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Measure out the 2 cups of Kodiak mix
  2. Pour the milk into the mix
  3. Add the cinnamon or chocolate chips
  4. Mix until you no longer see any powder

    5. If your mixture looks like this, you need to keep mixing

  5. Heat your stove to low or medium low heat, you don't ever want high heat for pancakes
  6. When your pan is warm, add butter and spread it all on the surface
  7. Add mixture to pan, not to much because you want it cook properly
